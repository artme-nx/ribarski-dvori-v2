Naša priča

Our story

Ista ruka,
ista vatra.

Same hands,
same fire.

Ribarski dvori su tu od 1991. Ne mijenjamo puno — ovdje se ne mijenja ono što radi. Riba stiže jutrom, peka se loži dan ranije, a vino teče onako kako Dalmacija i zna.

Ribarski Dvori have been here since 1991. We don't change much — here we don't change what works. Fish arrives in the morning, the peka is lit the day before, and wine flows the Dalmatian way.

Prvić je otok bez auta. Da dođu do nas, gosti vežu brod za naš molić i šetaju kroz uvalu. Baš onako kako treba biti.

Prvić is an island without cars. To reach us, guests tie their boat at our dock and walk through the bay. Exactly as it should be.

Dnevni ulov

Daily catch

01
Riba i plodovi mora
Fish & Seafood

Jutarnji ulov direktno na stol — gradele, buzara, na lešo, na brujet. Svaki dan drukčije, onako kako more dade. Svježe, bez pretenzija, s ukusom koji ne treba objašnjenja.

Morning catch straight to the table — grilled, in buzara, poached, in brujet. Different every day, as the sea gives. Fresh, unpretentious, with a flavour that needs no explanation.

02
Peka

Hobotnica, teletina, janjetina ili pivac — ispod peke, polako, dok ne bude savršeno. Peka se naručuje dan ranije. Ne možemo požuriti — ne trebamo.

Octopus, veal, lamb or chicken — under the bell, slowly, until perfect. Peka must be ordered one day in advance. We cannot rush it — we don't need to.

03
Vino i rakija
Wine & Spirits

Domaća dalmatinska kapljica, bez etiketa i bez viška. Točno onoliko koliko treba uz ribu i pogled na more. Loza, travarica, biska — kako se i doma pije.

Homegrown Dalmatian wine, no labels, no excess. Exactly what's needed with fish and a view of the sea. Loza, travarica, biska — just like home.
